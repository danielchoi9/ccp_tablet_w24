About Me

Hello! My name is Daniel Choi, a first year student for Michigan's MSI program. I'm currently going through the UI/UX track. I got my undergraduate degree from Michigan as well, in biopsychology and neuroscience. This is my test website for SI 539, and I'm excited to learn more in the future!

Most of my professional experiences have actually been in education and teaching, although I'm looking to get started in a more technology-focused work environment soon. I still love teaching, however, and I'd love to be a lecturer or professor in the future!

I have a wide array of hobbies. Working out is definitely my favorite one, but in my spare time, I also paint, watch shows and movies, play games, and cook!

I'm looking forward to learning a lot of new things this year. I am a little nervous about diving into a completely different field of study, but I'm sure that with hard work I'll be able to keep up.
